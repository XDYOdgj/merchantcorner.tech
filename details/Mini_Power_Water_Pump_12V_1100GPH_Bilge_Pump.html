Reliable & Portable Mini Water Pump

This compact mini water pump is the perfect solution for anyone looking for an efficient, space-saving way to remove water from various environments. Whether you're on the water or at home, its lightweight and portable design makes it easy to store and carry, ensuring it's always ready when you need it.

With a high flow rate of 1100GPH, this water transfer pump delivers fast and effective performance for boat bilge systems, seaplanes, motor homes, and even houseboats. The dual voltage support (12V/24V) ensures compatibility with different power sources, making it a versatile tool for both marine and domestic use.
